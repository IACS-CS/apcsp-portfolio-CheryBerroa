About Me

Hello! My name is Chery Berroa. I'm a student at Innovation Academy Charter School. I'm also a senior, making this my last year at IACS. I'm determined and hardworking in my classes, but outside of school, I'm actually a nice and outgoing person once you get to know me.

Hobbies

Interests
